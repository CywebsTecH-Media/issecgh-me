Staff SMS

The SMS module allows you to broadcast SMS (short messages) to staff members .

Send SMS messages to staff members

  1. Select the staff category.
  2. Click the Search button. Staff members belonging to the selected category are populated.
  3. Select the staff member/s. Selected staff members are highlighted. 
    • Click Ctrl+A to select all the staff members. 
    • Click the names of the staff members while pressing Ctrl to select multiple students. 
  4. Click the right arrow button. The selected staff members are shown on the Selected Staff Member(s) box.
  5. Enter the message in the Message textbox.  
  6. Click the Send button to send the message. 
Note:
  • SMS notifications can be sent to the parents of the students selected from folowing categories.
    • Admission no
    • Grade
    • Class
    •  Sport
    • Club and society
    • Prefect Type
    • Status (current, temporary leave, suspended)
    • Subject
    • Last name
  • Message should not exceed 160 characters.

Staff

  1. Select the student category.
  2. Click the Search button. Students belonging to the selected category are populated.
  3. Slect the student/s. Selected students are highlighted. 
    1. Click Ctrl+A to select all the students. 
    2. Click on students while pressing the Ctrl button to select multiple students. 
  4. Click the right arrow button. The selected students are shown on the Selected student(s) box.
  5. Enter the message in the Message textbox.  
  6. Click the Send button to send the message.
