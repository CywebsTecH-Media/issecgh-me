About Àkura


Àkura is a school management system that utilizes the latest technology to facilitate a new managerial experience for the principal and teachers of a school. Àkura also helps parents to be updated regarding the academic progress of their children by providing an effective parent–teacher interaction mechanism. 

Àkura has features to manage attendance, academic activities, co-curricular activities, religious activities and discipline within a school. The principal and teachers can monitor the progress of students at an individual level as well as at class and grade levels.
