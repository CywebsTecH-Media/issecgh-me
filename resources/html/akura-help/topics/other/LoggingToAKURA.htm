Logging in to Àkura

Navigation: Login page

You can log in to the Àkura school management system by entering the username and the password e-mailed to you by the system administrator. If you are logging in to Àkura for the first time, you have to change the default password that was e-mailed to you by the administrator. During the initial login, you will be prompted to set security questions as well. If you have forgotten your username or password, you can retrieve them through the Login page.

Note: You are allowed four attempts to enter the correct username and password. Following the fourth unsuccessful log in attempt, your account will be locked. Therefore, it is recommended to reset your password before making the fifth attempt. Once your account is locked, you will need to contact the administrator to have it unlocked.
