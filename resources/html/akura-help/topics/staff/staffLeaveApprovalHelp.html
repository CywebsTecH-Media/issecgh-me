Staff Leave Approval

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Staff Leave Approval 

Using this page, you can search for Staff Leave details and then approve or reject leave applied by the staff members.

  1. Enter the following details:
    • Leave Status (not mandatory)
    • EMP No (not mandatory)
  2. Click the Search button to view the search results.

Leave Approval Process

  1. Click the  button of the relevant leave record.
  2. Select a leave status from the radio buttons. 
  3. Provide a comment.
  4. Click Save to apply the selected leave status.
Note: Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory.
