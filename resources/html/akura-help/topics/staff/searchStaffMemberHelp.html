Search and Add New Staff Member

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Search Staff Member

Using this page, you can search for staff members and add new members. 

Search Staff Member

  1. Enter any of the following details:
    • Last Name or part of the Last Name 
    • Employee Number (EMP No)
    • Staff Type, i.e. Academic or Non-Academic using the radio buttons.
    • Staff Status, i.e. Present Staff or Past Staff using the drop-down menu.
  2. Click the Search button to view search results.
  3. Select the name of a staff member to view personal details.
  4. Navigate through the search result pages using the Previous and Next buttons.

Note: 

  • Click the Search button to view all staff members of the school.
  • Click the Staff Member Name or the Edit Staff Member button to edit the staff member details.
  • Staff members assigned to classes cannot be deleted.
  • To search for current staff members and past staff members select the relevant status from the Staff Status drop-down menu. 
  • Date Joined field cannot be changed later. 
  • Past staff members are shown with a View Staff Service button. 
  • Current staff members are shown with Terminate Staff, Edit Staff and Delete buttons.
  • Staff members utilized/allocated in the system cannot be deleted.

Add New Staff Member

  1. Click the Add New Staff Member button.
  2. Enter the Staff Member Details in the Staff Member Details page.
  3. Click Save to save the details, click Reset to reset the fields or click Cancel to cancel the add operation.
Note: Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory.
