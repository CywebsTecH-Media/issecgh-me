Staff Member Qualifications

Navigation: Select Staff Module->Search Staff Member->Edit->Qualifications

Using this page, you can manage the qualification details of staff members.

Educational Qualifications

Add Educational Qualifications

  1. To add the staff member's educational qualification information, click the Add button and fill in the required details.
  2. Click Save to save the details or click Cancel to cancel the add operation.

Edit Educational Qualifications

  1. To edit educational qualification information, click the Edit button in the row section.
  2. Make changes as required.
  3. Click Save to save the changes or click Cancel to cancel the changes.

Delete Educational Qualifications

  1. To delete educational qualification information, click the Delete button in the row section.
  2. A confirmation message is displayed. Click OK to delete the education qualification information or click Cancel to cancel the deletion.

Add Professional Qualifications

  1. To add the staff member's professional qualification information, click the Add button and fill in the required details.

Delete Professional Qualifications

Note:

  • If there are any existing staff member qualification details, the data is displayed in the page.
  • Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory.
  • Before adding the country, race, religion, civil status, section, staff category, nature of the appointment, classification of the appointment, staff service category, city make sure that the required reference data has been entered to the system through the Reference module.
  • Use the Calendar button to select a date.
  • Clicking the Home button will take you to the Homepage .
  • Clicking the Staff Search button will take you to the Staff Search page.
