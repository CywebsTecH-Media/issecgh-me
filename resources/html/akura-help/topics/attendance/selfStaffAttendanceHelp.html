Self Staff Attendance

Navigation: Select Attendance Module->Self Staff Attendance

Using this page, you can manually enter the staff attendance. 

Marking the arrival 

  1. Enter the Registration number of the staff member. 
  2. Click the Search button to view the details of a staff member with date/time of arrival.
  3. Click the Save button to mark the arrival. 

Marking the departure 

  1. Enter the Registration number of the staff member. 
  2. Click the Search button to view the details of a staff member with date/time of leave.
  3. Click the Save button to mark the departure. 
Notes:
  • Staff members on approved leave are notified in green. 
  • Staff members who have applied for leave are notified in yellow.
  • Attendance can only be marked for the current date.
  • Attendance can be marked for staff members on full day leave, in case they are to attend the school.  
  • Attendance should be marked for staff members on Half-Day leave. 
  • In case of multiple marking of departure, the last marking is saved as the final marking. 
  • Attendance cannot be marked for weekends or holidays. 
  • Holidays should be defined through the reference module, before marking attendance.
