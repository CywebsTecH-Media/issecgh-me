Manage Method of Travel

Navigation: Select Reference Module->Manage Method of Travel

Using this page, you can view, add, edit and delete data related to the method of travel.

View Methods of Travel

This is the homepage for the method of travel section. It displays a list of methods of travel available in the system.

Add New Method of Travel

  1. Click the Add New Method of Travel button.
  2. Enter the Method of Travel.
  3. Click the Save button to save the details or click Cancel to cancel the addition. 
Note: Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory.

Edit Method of Travel

  1. Click the Edit Method of Travel button of the relevant method of travel from the list to edit. The selected row is highlighted in yellow.
  2. Edit the data and click the Save button to save the changes or click Cancel to cancel the changes.

Delete Method of Travel

  1. Click the Delete button of a method of travel from the list to delete. The selected row is highlighted in yellow.
  2. A confirmation message is displayed. Click OK to confirm the deletion or click Cancel to cancel the deletion. 
Note: Data shared by other modules cannot be deleted.
