Manage Study Medium

Navigation: Select Reference Module->Manage Study Medium

Study mediums define the language of study in the system. Using this page, you can search for study mediums and then add, edit and delete study mediums. 

View Study Medium

This is the homepage for the Study Medium section. It displays a list of study mediums availablein the system.

Search Study Medium

  1. Enter the full name or partial name of a study medium.
  2. Click the Search button to get search results. 

Add New Study Medium

  1. Click the Add New Study Medium button.
  2. Enter the Study Medium name.
  3. Click the Save button to add the study medium or click Cancel to cancel the add operation. 
Note: Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory.

Edit Study Medium

  1. Click the Edit Study Medium button. The selected record is highlighted in yellow.
  2. Edit the Study Medium name.
  3. Click the Save button to save the changes or click Cancel to cancel the changes. 

Delete Study Medium

  1. Click the Delete button. The selected record is highlighted in yellow.
  2. A confirmation message is displayed. Click the OK button to confirm the deletion or click Cancel to cancel the deletion. 
Note: Data shared by other modules cannot be deleted.
